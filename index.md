## Who is Molley for?

Molley is designed for **solo entrepreneurs**, **product managers**, **sales teams**, and **research professionals** who need a unified platform to:

- **Research** markets, competitors, and user needs
- **Document** product requirements and specifications
- **Track** features from ideation to release
- **Grow** through organized outreach and customer management
- **Collect** feedback to continuously improve

Whether you're a one-person startup or a growing team, Molley scales with your needs while keeping everything connected and AI-powered.
